About

  • The predictor utilizes the award database from the SBIR program to predict future funding allocation for different research areas. Three machine learning models are applied individually and the one resulting in the smallest error is used for predictions. The goal is to help the applicants (i.e. small businesses) to make decision during project development.
  • This predictor was constructed by Ling Yang. You are welcome to connect with Ling via email (ling.yang@mg.thedataincubator.com) or on LinkedIn.
